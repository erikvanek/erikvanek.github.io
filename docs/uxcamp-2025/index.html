The Key Takeaway

Preparation vs. Execution

  • This was probably a one-off event
  • The ratio of preparation to execution was outrageous
  • I don't want to count the hours we spent on it
  • Every World Caf√© script was punctual and precisely crafted
  • Every template was carefully designed
  • Every instruction was tested and refined

Why This Matters

  • Environment not used to working this way
  • If you take the patience and invest the time
  • If you prepare properly and meticulously
  • You can get breakthrough results even in traditionally resistant environments
  • And hopefully, everyone enjoys the process

What We Learned

  • Context adaptation is crucial - government stakeholders need different approaches
  • Leadership participation makes all the difference
  • Small, actionable changes resonate more than big visions
  • Extraordinary preparation enables ordinary people to do extraordinary things
  • Co-creation works - even in the most traditional hierarchical structures

For Fellow Designers

  • Don't underestimate the preparation-to-execution ratio
  • Adapt your facilitation style to your audience
  • Seek leadership buy-in and active participation
  • Focus on small, implementable changes
  • Human-centered design works everywhere - including government
  • Sometimes accidents lead to the best opportunities
