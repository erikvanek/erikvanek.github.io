AI-assisted social-health system navigation

The goal was to help people in vulnerable life situations find the most appropriate form of help. This case study explores how we could leverage capabilities of largue language models to build a easy to use tool that helps to minimize the time to first contact when experiencing difficult life situations.

Result

Main purpose of this journey was to conclude a solid, tested and data-backed prototype of a tool that might help users on their journey to find the help they need. Clickeable prototype can be clicked through here.
