PM Challenges

Navigating Regulatory Environments

  • Different countries have unique regulatory requirements (EU regulations, content restrictions, etc.)
  • Requires understanding of local laws and adaptation of product strategies

Resource Competition

  • Internal competition for finite resources: financial, human, screen space, team priorities
  • "The bigger the org, the more eyes on you"

Handling Unpredictability

  • PMs must tackle numerous unforeseen challenges
  • Requires adaptability and contingency planning

Effective PM Strategies

Strategic Focus Selection

  • Concentrate efforts on high-impact initiatives — doing the right things
  • Evaluate based on available resources, approval processes, political landscape, external competitive factors

Metrics-Driven Persuasion

  • Connect specific product metrics to broader organizational goals — use as a persuasion tool in cross-team discussions
  • When direct measurement is impossible, develop proxy metrics
  • Example: where A/B testing can't establish correlations, employ data science for deeper analysis

Early Cross-Team Alignment

  • Proactively discuss upcoming initiatives with stakeholder teams
  • Set expectations and reach agreement on success metrics upfront
  • Address potential metric conflicts: "What if an increase in our metric causes a decrease in their metric?"

Multi-Perspective Analysis

  • Analyze situations through the lens of other teams: their objectives, metrics, operational workflows

Diligent Internal Review

  • Engage with compliance teams early: safety, privacy, financial impact, legal
  • Their role is preventative: "To ensure nothing goes wrong and no problems appear in the future that I can't envision"

Key Takeaways

  • Success requires both tactical skill and strategic thinking
  • Effective use of metrics is critical for cross-team persuasion and alignment
  • Early stakeholder engagement prevents roadblocks later
