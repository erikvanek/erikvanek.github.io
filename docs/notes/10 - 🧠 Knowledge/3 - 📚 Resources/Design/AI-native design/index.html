Notes from a podcast about designing and building with AI (context: Lovable — fastest growing startup in European history).

  • The biggest enabler is talking to users and customers on repeat — constant learning about the users and the market
    • designers (and anyone with research skills) have a superpower — knowing how to research people helps us achieve our goals
  • Don't fall in love with your ideas — they're going to be wrong. Be willing to be wrong
  • If code becomes the source of truth it changes the game massively compared to Figma being in the center
  • Design process big to small: hierarchy first, information within that, then content design, then layout
    • if it's related to layout → reach for Figma; if it's interactive → go for Lovable
  • When vibe-prompting: treat AI as a creative partner during a jam session — act like a design director with contextual prompts like make it more like art-deco meets Wes Anderson to explore visual and content forms you wouldn't think of by yourself
  • Lovable is preparing MCP integration
