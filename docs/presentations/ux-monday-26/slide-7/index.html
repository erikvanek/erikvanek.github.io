"Have you been enjoying your job more or less since adopting AI tools?"

— Lenny Rachitsky / x.com/lennysan

Lenny Rachitsky ran three separate X polls: "Have you been enjoying your job more or less since adopting AI tools?" Engineers: 72.2% more, 10.7% less, 17.1% no change. PMs: 70.2% more, 11.1% less, 18.7% no change. Designers: 55.0% more, 18.2% less, 26.8% no change. The designer number is the one worth naming out loud. Engineers and PMs are a headline - but designers are meaningfully different. 18.2% saying less is not noise. Don't editorialize too much here. Just let the gap land. Your audience is mostly designers - they'll feel it. Transition: "So something is different about how this shift is hitting our discipline. That's partly what this talk is about."
