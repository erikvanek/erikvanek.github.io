Piloting a new childcare service

(More details soon)

Project goals

Pilot a not-yet established childcare service with selected participants

The main aim of the project is to pilot a gradually emerging childcare service called Sousedské dětské skupiny. The rigid structure and rules obsessed environment turns this problem into a chicken-egg situation. The pilot programme can’t be started until the legislation is signed off and vice versa. That brings severe roadblocks and obstacles throughout the project lifetime.

Increase design awareness at the ministry

It is one of the first in-house led projects that involves designers’ expertise. One of the key goals is to share knowledge among colleagues about design approaches and techniques. We’re focusing strongly on co-creating with the rest of the project team to maximize the possibility to share knowledge.

Help the organization understand better user's needs

The project invests a lot of resources into understanding users’ needs to be able to configure service’s parameters correctly. We’re using a variation of methods like in-depth interviews, fake-door tests or ethnographic probes to understand what are the needs, motivations and expectations of our target groups.

Share lessons learnt and key findings with the innovation department

The value of the project is to share back with the innovation department what are the main obstacles to bring more innovation to the standard ways of working of the organization. We’re continuously documenting and sharing peculiarities and constraints that the ministry’s environment brings.

Key activities

  • Conducting user research
  • Prototyping service touchpoints and its aspects
  • Design advocation and “selling” its value and benefits
  • Increasing organization's design maturity
  • Helping the team to use appropriate collaboration and project management tools and best practices
  • Workshop and round tables preparation and facilitation
