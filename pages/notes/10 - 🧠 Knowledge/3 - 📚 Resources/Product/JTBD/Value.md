- instead of attaching value to what products *are*, we should attach value to what they allow users to *achieve*
- getting something done is more important than features
- value is pretty ambiguous and **means different things to different people or stakeholders**
- products and services promise solving people's problems and fulfilling their wishes — that plus _something extra_ allows to **bring value**
