- good product teams tackle [big risks first](https://www.svpg.com/beyond-lean-and-agile/), especially value risk

## SVPG taxonomy
1. **Value risk** — whether customers will buy it or users will choose to use it *(biggest risk of all)*
2. **Business viability risk** — whether this solution also works for the various aspects of our business *(second most important — if not tackled, the org bleeds out)*
3. **Usability risk** — whether users can figure out how to use it
4. **Feasibility risk** — whether engineers can build what we need with the time, skills and technology we have

## IDEO taxonomy
- **Desirability** — combines customer value and usability
- **Viability**
- **Feasibility**
