The goal is to analyze current state of processes used in an organization.

There are two main types:
- **as-is** — analyzing currently used processes; says where I am
- **to be** — enhancing the as-is version with changes needed to reach new organizational goals; says where I want to be

## Tools
- LucidChart
- BPMN editor (draw.io)

## Process
- Start by compiling a list of all products and services to get a clear outline of the business's value chain
- Identify all processes the company uses to generate those products and services, ordered chronologically
- Note when each process starts and ends, and identify which teams or individuals are involved
