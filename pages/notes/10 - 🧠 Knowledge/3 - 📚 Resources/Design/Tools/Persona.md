- _not a stereotype but an archetype_
- should be based on research and evolve over time
- help teams get on the same page and build empathy with users

## What to include
- portrait image
- name
- demographics (beware of stereotypes!)
- a representative quote
- mood images — persona in their natural context
- description — characteristics, attitudes, skills, needs, expectations, tech-savviness
- statistics — summarize relevant quantitative info
- template: [Persona Canvas](https://www.designabetterbusiness.tools/tools/persona-canvas)

## Archetypes
- focus more on behavior rather than demographics
- not a single character but a pattern
