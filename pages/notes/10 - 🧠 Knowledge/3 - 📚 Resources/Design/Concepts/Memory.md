- let people rest (and even sleep) if you want them to remember information
- information in the middle of a presentation is the least likely to be remembered
- you can remember things you see (visual memory) better than things you hear or read as words

## Effects
- **Recency effect** — remembering the end of what was talked about during a presentation
- **Suffix effect** — remembering the beginning of what was talked about before being interrupted by a distraction (e.g. a phone)
