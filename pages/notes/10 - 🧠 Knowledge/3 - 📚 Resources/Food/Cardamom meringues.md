# Ingredients
- 6 egg yolks (190g)
- 1.5 teaspoons of lemon juice
- 400g of caster sugar
- 0.25 teaspoon of salt
- 0.25 teaspoon of cayenne
- .5 teaspoon of ground cardamom
- .5 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
- 100g of toasted and chopped pumpkin seeds
